Hi there! This is the Responsive community, a group of friends who somehow make an awesome podcast for an awesome community!

Community

The Responsive community are a bunch of fellow programmers who enjoy talking, sharing projects, and helping eachother out. That said, we do have pleanty of unrelated fun too (we're only human).

You can join us on the community Discord! Feel free to start a convocation at any time, we don't bite, as long as you're not a pop-tart that is...

Podcasts

And if you like the community, you'll probably like our podcasts too! They're not highly edited and cut to perfection, but instead just a few friends talking to eachother about tech.

You can find out more on Anchor or search Responsive on you're favorite podcasting platform.
